🎯The Problem

We are living through the greatest attention crisis 
in human history.

The average human attention span has dropped from 
12 seconds in 2000 to just 8 seconds today — 
shorter than a goldfish. And students are hit hardest.

Reels. YouTube Shorts. TikTok. Every app is 
engineered by teams of PhDs to hijack your 
dopamine system and make 3 seconds feel boring. 
Students are now conditioned to expect constant 
stimulation — making 25 minutes of uninterrupted 
studying feel almost impossible.

The numbers tell the story:
→ Students check their phone 150+ times per day
→ It takes 23 minutes to regain deep focus 
   after a single distraction
→ 80% of students report they cannot study 
   for more than 10 minutes without 
   reaching for their phone
→ The average student actually focuses for 
   only 65% of their "study time"

Traditional study methods offer zero accountability. 
Nothing stops a student from picking up their phone, 
zoning out, or mindlessly scrolling Reels while 
their textbook sits open in front of them.

The result? Hours of fake studying. Failed exams. 
Growing frustration. And a generation of brilliant 
students who cannot unlock their own potential 
because their attention has been stolen from them 
— one 15-second video at a time.

💡 The Solution — LockIn

LockIn is an AI-powered study monitoring web 
application that fights back against the attention 
economy — bringing intelligent, real-time 
accountability to every study session.

It acts as a silent AI guardian — always watching, 
always nudging you back to focus.

LockIn understands that the problem is not 
willpower. The problem is that billion-dollar 
companies have spent years training your brain 
to crave distraction. You cannot out-willpower 
an algorithm. You need a system.

LockIn is that system.

When a student starts a session:

→ The webcam activates and AI models load 
   directly in the browser
→ BlazeFace model continuously checks if 
   the student's face is visible and present
→ COCO-SSD object detection scans for 
   phone usage every single second
→ If the phone is picked up — an instant 
   triple-beep alarm fires followed by 
   a voice alert
→ If the student leaves the frame — 
   a warning triggers immediately
→ A live Focus Score updates in real-time 
   based on distraction frequency
→ A Pomodoro timer structures study into 
   focused 25-minute sprints — training 
   the brain to rebuild its attention span
→ At session end, all stats are saved — 
   duration, subject, distraction count, 
   and focus score

The dashboard gives students a complete picture 
of their study habits — total hours, average 
focus score per subject, distraction trends, 
and daily streaks that build long-term discipline.

Over time LockIn doesn't just monitor — 
it rewires. By making distractions immediately 
consequential and visible, it gradually rebuilds 
the deep focus muscle that Reels and Shorts 
have eroded. Students who use it consistently 
report being able to focus for longer periods 
naturally — because the habit of catching 
themselves gets internalized.

Privacy First — Non-Negotiable

Unlike screen-recording or surveillance apps, 
LockIn processes everything locally in the 
browser using TensorFlow.js.

→ Zero video is ever uploaded, recorded, 
   or stored on any server
→ All AI inference happens on your device
→ Only anonymous session statistics 
   are saved to the database
→ Your face never leaves your device. Period.

In a world where your data is the product, 
LockIn takes the opposite stance — 
your privacy is the feature.
