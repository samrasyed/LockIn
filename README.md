🎯The Problem

The modern student faces an attention crisis. Research shows 
that the average student checks their phone 150+ times per day, 
and it takes over 20 minutes to regain deep focus after each 
distraction. Traditional study methods offer no accountability 
— there is nothing stopping a student from picking up their 
phone, zoning out, or simply walking away from their desk 
while pretending to study.

The result? Hours of "studying" that produce almost zero 
retention, failed exams, and a growing frustration with 
the inability to concentrate.

The Solution — LockIn

LockIn is an AI-powered study monitoring web application 
that brings intelligent, real-time accountability to every 
study session. It acts as a silent AI guardian — always 
watching, always nudging you back to focus.

When a student starts a session:

→ The webcam activates and AI models load in the browser
→ BlazeFace model continuously checks if the student's 
   face is visible and present
→ COCO-SSD object detection model scans for phone usage 
   every second
→ If the phone is picked up — an instant triple-beep alarm 
   fires followed by a voice alert
→ If the student leaves the frame — a warning triggers 
   immediately
→ A live Focus Score updates in real-time based on 
   distraction frequency
→ A Pomodoro timer helps structure study into focused 
   25-minute sprints
→ At session end, all stats are saved — duration, subject, 
   distraction count, and focus score

The dashboard gives students a bird's eye view of their 
study habits — total hours, average focus score per subject, 
distraction trends, and daily streaks.

 Privacy First

Unlike screen-recording or surveillance apps, LockIn 
processes everything locally in the browser using TensorFlow.js. 
No video is ever uploaded, recorded, or stored. Only anonymous 
session statistics are saved to the database.

Why It Matters?

LockIn is not just a productivity tool — it is a 
behavioral intervention. By making distractions visible 
and immediately consequential through alerts, it trains 
students to build better study habits over time. The focus 
score gamifies concentration, making students want to improve 
their numbers — turning studying into a measurable, 
rewarding challenge.

In a world where every app is designed to steal your 
attention, LockIn gives it back.
